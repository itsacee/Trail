Meet Your Coach

Elijah Alexander

I'm Elijah Alexander, founder of AP Academy here in Oklahoma City — and I've hit at every level of college baseball. Two seasons at Connors State, where I batted .434 with 17 home runs, 47 doubles and 129 RBIs in 113 games. A season with the Oklahoma State Cowboys (2024–25). And at the University of Central Oklahoma, where I hit .333 with 9 home runs. I'm not teaching from a textbook — I'm teaching what I do in the box every day.

Every lesson is one-on-one and built around your player. We find what's holding them back, attack it with drills they actually enjoy, and send them home with a plan. No cookie-cutter programs — just honest feedback, real reps, and progress you can see.

FAQ

Common Questions

What ages do you train?

Players from age 7 through high school. Every session is adjusted to your player's age, skill level, and goals — a 8U beginner and a varsity starter get very different lessons.

Where are lessons held?

Training is at 3701 S Bryant Ave, Del City, OK 73115 — just off I-40 in the OKC metro. Questions before you book? Call or text (405) 819-4401.

How long is a session?

Every session is a full hour of focused work — no rushed 30-minute cage rentals. Group sessions are one hour total, with both players training together.

What should my player bring?

Glove, bat, helmet, cleats or turf shoes, and a water bottle. If you're missing anything, let me know before the session — I've got extras.

How does booking and payment work?

Right here on this site — scroll to Book Now, pick a day and time, enter your player's name, and pay securely through Stripe. Your card is never stored by us. The spot is yours as soon as payment goes through.

What's your cancellation policy?

Life happens — reschedule free of charge with at least 24 hours notice. Same-day cancellations may forfeit the session fee.
